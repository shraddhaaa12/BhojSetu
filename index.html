About BhojSetu

BhojSetu is a food donation platform designed to reduce food wastage by connecting restaurants, households and event organizers with NGOs and community organizations. The platform helps ensure that surplus food reaches people in need instead of being discarded.

Why This Problem Matters

Every day large quantities of food are wasted at restaurants, weddings and social events while many people struggle to access nutritious meals. If this issue is not addressed, food wastage, hunger and environmental impact will continue to grow.
